The Beginning:

Since the late 1990s, Google has forever changed the internet. A powerful search engine that finds exactly what you want, when you want it, but how does it work? Who was behind this creation? How has their influence forever changed the internet?

The story of Google starts in 1995 at Stanford University, “Larry Page was considering Stanford for grad school and Sergey Brin, a student there, was assigned to show him around” (Google). Both Larry Page and Sergey Brin “were intrigued with the idea of enhancing the ability to extract meaning from mass of data accumulated on the Internet” (Encyclopædia Britannica). Their idea was to create a system that could search the web and “crawl the Internet to determine which pages were linking to other pages” (The Verge).
